Terms

Term Definition
Champions The playable characters in the game
Lane The main paths that extend from the nexus. There are three lanes: Top, Mid, and Bot
Jungle The area inbetween lanes. In the jungle are neutral monsters that the jungler kills to gain exp and gold
Ganking When a jungler goes into a lane to help a laner kill an enemy or provide pressure
Rune Abilities players can set before game to enhance gameplay
Item The things you buy in game to make your champion stronger
